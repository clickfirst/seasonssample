GOVERNMENT PROGRAMS CAN HELP YOU PAY FOR LONG TERM CARE

Essentially there are Two Government Programs designed to assist us with paying for the cost of our long term care.   
Medicaid ICP- Institutional Care Program

( NO! , ITS NOT JUST FOR POOR PEOPLE) 
Veterans Pension with the Aid and Attendance Benefit ( Wartime Veterans and their Spouses) 

Self Pay

Liquidation of current savings and other personal assets: This includes using Qualified Funds, IRAs, 401K, Non Qualified investment funds made with after tax dollars and/or the sale of your home or other real estate.

What should be noted here;  in addition to the monthly cost of care, there can be many ADDITIONAL HIDDEN COSTS that are not so apparent when calculating the True FINAL Cost of Care.

The following are a few such considerations.

Loss of Interest CD's
Potential Surrender Charges on Insurance products : Annuities both Fixed, Indexed or Variable
Loss of Death Benefit on Life Insurance
Capital Gains
Income Taxes
Loss of future Return On Investment( ROI)

Someone Else Pays

The questions is who? Is it another family member, an insurance company, a Government entity or a combination of all of these? 

1. If another Family members pays - fantastic. Most people don't have this option. 

2. Long Term Care Insurance pays. 

A. Unfortunately this will in all likely- hood NOT be sufficient to cover the Total Monthly Cost of Care of Skilled Nursing Care. In that case, see the self pay options.
  
B. Long term care when combined with net monthly " MAY" be sufficient to cover the majority of the cost for Assisted Living Care.  

3. Government Entitlements pay

A. BUT unlike Social Security and Medicare where EVERYONE Qualifies , we just have to LIVE LONG ENOUGH.
 
B. Government LONG TERM CARE benefits are MEANS based programs , simply stated there are INCOME and ASSET restrictions both of which must be met to Qualify for these benefits.
 
C. IMPORTANT NOTE: with proper Estate Planning ALMOST everyone can qualify for these Government Entitlements regardless of their current Income and Assets. CLICK HERE TO FIND OUT HOW you too or your loved ones can Qualify for these benefits.
